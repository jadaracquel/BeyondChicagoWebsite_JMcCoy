CHICAGO TOURS & ATTRACTIONS

It’s no surprise that Chicago is one of the finest tourist destinations in the country. There are miles of beautiful Chicago beaches, Chicago museums that rank among the world's best and the friendliest city- dwellers out there. It’s really hard to visit Chicago and know the best tours and attractions to check out during your stay. Some say it’s a huge city, but actually, it turns out that the city isn’t quite as big as it looks or feels! With that being said, we’ve rounded up some of the city’s most iconic attractions and top tours to add to your itinerary.
