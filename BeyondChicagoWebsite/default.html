~Experience Big-City Excitement and Country Charms~


This diverse region offers travelers a bit of everything. In Chicago, the cultural offerings range from world-renowned museums to amazing architecture and cutting-edge theater. Shop Downtown along the Magnificient Mile and in hip neighborhoods, dine at award-winning restaurants and experience legendary nightlife. Go beyond the city limits and ride the wild roller coasters at a massive theme park, dive into splashy water resorts and hike among awesome waterfalls at a scenic state park. Even farther out, charming small towns encourage visitors to stroll the historic streets and slow down their lives just a little.
